Main Page

Mumbai

A city is known by many names such as the city of dreams, India’s financial center, Mayanagri, and the one that never sleeps - Mumbai is a city full of extraordinary experiences. Offering a visual treat to Gothic architecture lovers, Mumbai is sprinkled with well-index architectural wonder’s from its British era days. To experience the charm of a fast-paced megacity, board a local train during peak hours to know Mumbai from a closeup.

Mahabaleshwar

Popular for its evergreen timberlands, breathtaking landscapes, ancient temples, and waterfalls, Mahabaleshwar is a famous hill station nestled amid the scenic Western Ghats. Trekking to Pratapgarh Fort, boating in Venna Lake, exploring Rajpuri Caves, clicking Insta-worthy pictures, and treating your taste buds with freshly-plucked strawberries are some of the must-do things in Mahabaleshwar.

Khandala

Thanks to a popular Bollywood song, Khandala became another famous place in Maharashtra that is located quite close to Lonavala. During the monsoon season, the place turns lush green although, the weather here remains pleasant throughout the year. Khandala offers views of stunning valleys and picturesque peaks along with pristine lakes and beautiful waterfalls. Della Adventure Park, Sunil’s Celebrity Wax Museum, Aadishakti Mata Temple, and Karla and Bhanja Caves are some of the must-visit attractions in Khandala.

Lonavala

Offering a quick getaway from the rushed city life, Lonavala is located between Mumbai and Pune. Famous for its scenic drive from both sides, Pune is known for its mouth-watering sweet snack called Chikki. The long tunnels and scenic roads on the Pune-Mumbai Expressway will make you come back to Lonavala again and again. Surrounded by some of the best places to visit in Maharashtra, Lonavala is a very small yet beautiful hill station. Lohagarh Fort, Tiger’s Point, Kune Falls, Visapur Fort, Bhaja Caves, and Shree Narayani Dham Temple are some of the must-visit places in Lonavala.

Panchgani

Known for its lush green forests, beautiful waterfalls, and juicy strawberries, Panchgani is a popular hill station that is enclosed by five hills. Panchgani would be a perfect pick for you if you love nature vacations. Don’t forget to visit the Kaas Plateau - a UNESCO World Heritage Site when you are in Panchgani. In the months of August and September, Kaas Plateau and surrounding grasslands turn into a sea of blooming flowers.

Alibaug

A small coastal town in the Konkan region of Maharashtra, Alibaug is popular as ‘Mini-Goa’. You can visit many forts and temples apart from spending time on the serene beaches of Alibaug. With Kolaba Fort and stunning views of the sunrise and sunset, Alibaug Beach is the most-visited beach in town. For photography enthusiasts, Nagaon Beach and Kihim Beach are perfect spots.

Karnala

A treat for architecture enthusiasts and nature lovers, Karnala is a beautiful city in the Rajgarh district. The place offers much respite from the summer heat and looks lush green during the monsoon season. The index attractions of this place are Karnala Fort and Karnala Bird Sanctuary. The bird sanctuary is home to several wild animals and over 220 species of avifauna.

Satara

One of the most important places to visit in Maharashtra, Satara is located near the confluence of Venna and Krishna rivers. Apart from the ancient caves, historical ruins, and forts, Satara is known for its natural beauty including gushing waterfalls, gigantic mountains, and pristine lakes. Thoseghar Falls, Natraj Mandir, Natskies Observatory, Ajinkyatara Fort, Lingamala Falls, and Mayani Bird Sanctuary are some of the best places to visit in Satara.

Ratnagiri

The home of the world’s tastiest mangoes, Alphonso, Ratnagiri is blessed with nature’s bounty and is surrounded by Sahyadri mountain ranges on one side and the Arabian Sea on the other. Housing many beaches, lighthouses, and ports, it is known as the port town of Maharashtra. The beautiful mangroves, gushing waterfalls, and sandy shores make it even more gorgeous than it already is. Jaigarh Fort and Lighthouse, Ganpatipule Beach, Swayambu Ganpati Temple, and Thiba Palace are some of the best places in Ratnagiri.

Khapoli

A popular weekend getaway from Pune and Mumbai, Khopoli is an industrial city in the Raigarh district of Maharashtra. For those looking to spend time in a peaceful place amidst nature, Khapoli is a perfect destination. One of the best entertainment destinations in India, Imagica Amusement Theme Park, is situated in Khapoli.
